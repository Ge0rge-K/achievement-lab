# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant code of conduct. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.

## Enforcement
If you experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers via a GitHub issue or discussion.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
